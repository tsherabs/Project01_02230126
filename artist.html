About

Thelungten is a groundbreaking artist who brings the ancient musical traditions of Tibet to modern audiences. Born in the highlands of Tibet and trained in traditional instruments from a young age, Thelungten has developed a unique sound that respects tradition while embracing contemporary influences.

Thelungten continues to push the boundaries of world music while serving as a cultural ambassador, sharing the rich heritage of Tibetan musical traditions with global audiences.
